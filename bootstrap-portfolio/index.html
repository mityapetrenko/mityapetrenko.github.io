About Me
Welcome to my page! My name is Dmitriy Petrenko. I have been a dental hygienist for 10 years. I live and work in Shakopee MN. I have lived in Shakopee for the past 5 years. My wife and I have two kids and a dog. My free time is filled with enjoying time with family and friends, gardening and enjoying nature.

Working as a dental hygienist taught me many valueable lessons. I have learned to manage my time to be as efficient and producting during the workday. Working in a dental office has taught me to be dependable. Knowing that I have patients who are counting on me to show up, pushed me to have excellent attendance at work.
